# Document Field Extractor Examples

## Example 1

User ask:

`Pull out the names, dates, invoice total, and payment terms from this file.`

Example output:

- Vendor name: Northbridge Supplies
- Invoice date: 12 March 2026
- Invoice total: AUD 4,280.00
- Payment terms: Net 30 days

## Example 2

User ask:

`Extract the approval conditions and deadlines from this memo.`

Example output:

- Approval condition: Final sign-off requires manager and finance approval
- Submission deadline: 5:00 PM on 18 April 2026
- Dependency: Supporting receipts must be attached before review
