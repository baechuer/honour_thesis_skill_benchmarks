# News Briefing Writer Examples

## Example 1

User ask:

`Turn these two news snippets about an AI company partnership and new product release into a short briefing.`

Example output:

- Topic: AI company partnership and product release
- Key development: The company announced a new strategic partnership alongside a product launch, combining a business expansion move with a fresh product push.
- Why it matters: The pairing suggests the company is trying to accelerate adoption and market visibility rather than making a standalone announcement.
- What to watch next: Customer uptake, rollout details, pricing implications, and whether competitors respond.

Good output shape:

- Topic: AI company expansion
- Key development: partnership + release
- Why it matters: broader distribution, competitive signal
- What to watch next: adoption, pricing, market response

## Example 2

User ask:

`Write a quick briefing from these articles about a cybersecurity incident affecting a major software vendor.`

Example output:

- Topic: Cybersecurity incident affecting a major software vendor
- Key development: The vendor disclosed a security incident that may affect customers using a widely deployed software product.
- Why it matters: Customers may need to assess exposure quickly, and the incident could have broader ecosystem consequences if critical systems are involved.
- What to watch next: Scope clarification, remediation guidance, customer advisories, and any indication of ongoing exploitation.

Good output shape:

- Topic: software vendor incident
- Key development: what happened
- Why it matters: customer risk, ecosystem impact
- What to watch next: remediation, official updates, scope clarification
