# Data Analysis For Root Cause Diagnosis Examples

## Example 1

User ask:

`Use this spreadsheet to help me understand what is driving the drop in conversion.`

Example output:

- Main issue: Conversion fell sharply in the most recent two periods.
- Likely driver or drivers: The decline appears concentrated in mobile traffic from one campaign source.
- Supporting evidence: Desktop conversion remained stable, while mobile conversion and checkout completion both worsened in the same segment.
- Uncertainty or alternative explanation: The shift may also reflect a tracking change, not only real behavior.
- Next check: Verify whether the mobile landing-page or campaign tagging changed during the affected period.

## Example 2

User ask:

`Why does this region suddenly look worse than the others?`

Example output:

- Main issue: The South region now has the weakest margin despite similar revenue movement.
- Likely driver or drivers: Higher return volume and discounting appear to be the main contributors.
- Supporting evidence: The region shows a simultaneous rise in returns and a larger average discount per order.
- Uncertainty or alternative explanation: A product-mix change may also be contributing.
- Next check: Break the region down by product category to isolate where the margin loss is concentrated.
