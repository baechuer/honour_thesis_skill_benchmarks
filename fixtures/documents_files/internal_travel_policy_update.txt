Internal Travel Policy Update
Effective date: 1 May 2026

This update applies to domestic and international work travel booked through the Operations team.

Travel requests must be submitted at least five working days before departure unless the trip is classified as urgent by the employee's manager. Manager approval is required before a request is sent to Finance for final approval.

Employees must attach itemized receipts for transport, accommodation, and meals within ten calendar days of returning. Reimbursements may be delayed if receipts are missing, illegible, or submitted after the deadline.

Trips above AUD 1,500 require Finance review even if the travel is urgent. Personal upgrades, companion expenses, and unapproved itinerary changes will not be reimbursed.

Questions about exceptions should be sent to ops-travel@example.com.
